🏛️ About Us

Sanjeevan Polytechnic, established in 2015, is a proud extension of the visionary Sanjeevan Group of Institutions. Founded with the aim of imparting quality technical education in a dynamic and industry-driven environment, the polytechnic has quickly emerged as a center of excellence.

With a focus on hands-on learning, innovation, and skill development, it nurtures future-ready diploma engineers. Sanjeevan Polytechnic continues to uphold the group's legacy of educational empowerment, especially in rural and semi-urban region of Panhala.

🎯 Our Mission

To cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and character in our students while preparing them for successful careers and meaningful lives.

We strive to create an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity and promotes lifelong learning.
